Services

Nwazet is the creator of the HyGroBot project. At Nwazet, we design custom electronics and enclosures around the Raspberry Pi, Python, and our own Open Source libraries. We can help you with defining requirements, building prototypes and guiding you through the process of transforming your ideas into industrial designs and production.

We welcome business-related inquiries regarding HyGroBot applications but please keep in mind that Federal regulations forbid us from giving advice on growing 'illegal' plants. Even though we disagree with such non-sense and oppose it with all legal means at our disposal, we still abide by the law. Thank you for understanding.
