HyGrowBot v1 being dismantled... to become part of HyGrowBot v3

Today, the [HyGrowBot v1][1] prototype was taken offline after 9 months of testing and experimenting. We learned a huge amount about bootstrapping a successful small scale hydroponic garden with HyGrowBot v1 but now is the time to take the experiment to whole new level. So, we're dismantling the v1 prtotype and we'll be re-using all of its parts in the process of building the HyGrowBot v3 prototype, with exciting new plants and on a much larger scale this time. This will enable us to test the pre-production version of the system we'll be releasing this Fall. The HyGrowBot v2 prototype that we showed at Hempfest 2013 last month will play a key role in this new garden as it will be nurturing a mother plant :) As usual, we'll document the process with pictures and video tutorials of the setup, code updates as we add features, starting with monitoring and controlling CO2 levels and managing a water reservoir feeding two separate areas of the garden. Cheers, -The HyGrowBot Team [1]: http://open.sen.se/sensemeters/tab/2081/
